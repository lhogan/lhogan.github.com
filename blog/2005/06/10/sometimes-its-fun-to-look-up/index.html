Sometimes It's Fun to Look Up!

Jun 10th, 2005

This is kind of cool. Myself and Dion were walking across campus to get to Bitter’s for some trivia action (we came fifth of eight, BTW… not bad for my first time in a while) last night around 7:30pm, and I looked up towards the Confederation Building and thought, “Is that a biplane? Are we in a time warp or something?”. Turns out, I may have been right… about the biplane, not the time warp (though I have been seeing all these singers from decades gone on TV).
