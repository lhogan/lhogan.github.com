Happiness Is...

May 18th, 2005

… sipping your latte, dunking you vanilla-almond biscotti in the comfy chairs at Starbucks while Dave Matthews plays (over the speakers). God, I’ve become pretentious!

But seriously, I’m having an up-and-down kind of week, so it was nice to spend some time “chilaxin’” as the kids say, with my love who has an interview today. I’m trying to tie up all loose ends, catch up and be a responsible adult. Speaking of which, good essay in today’s Globe and Mail on what happens to all that junk belonged to you that your parents saved - I know this day is coming for me. And my parents are pack rats extreme. It’s already happened for Brian.

But have a good hump-day. Long weekend coming!!
